The Map
This map shows the forest health evaluation results of 180 plot in Clumber park in the form of an choropleth map. The legend in the lower right corner shows the Forest ecosystem health index (FEHI) of each plot:
0-40 Unhealthy Forest
40-60 Sub-healthy Forest
60-80 Healthy Forest
80-100 Very healthy Forest

Hover a specific plot, the right info window will display the detailed data of each plot. Click on a specific plot to enlarge the area.

The Data
The data of this map comes from a study on the health of trees in 180 plots of Clumber Park. The study evaluates the forest health conditions from three aspects: forest vigour, forest organization and forest resistance. Click the button in the navigation bar to get more related information.

The forest health problem
Abiotic and biotic disturbances have major impacts on the health and vitality of the world’s forests and can result in substantial economic and environmental losses. They can have adverse effects on tree growth and survival, yield and quality of wood and non-wood products, wildlife habitat, recreation and scenic and cultural values.
